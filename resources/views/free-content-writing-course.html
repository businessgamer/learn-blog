Why Technical SEO Matters

Before we get into the details of this course, let's clarify why technical SEO is so important. In the digital sphere, search engines like Google play a vital role in determining the visibility of your website. Technical SEO focuses on the foundation of your online presence, addressing the backend elements that impact your website's search engine rankings. This includes aspects like site speed, mobile-friendliness, structured data, and more.

A strong understanding of technical SEO is the key to unlocking higher rankings, increased organic traffic, and a better user experience on your website. With the right techniques and practices, you can ensure that search engines / and rank your site favorably, bringing more potential customers to your virtual doors.
